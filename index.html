About Me

My Fashion Journey

I'm Dinah Brako — originally from Cape Coast and now based in Aplaku. As a student and fashion entrepreneur, I balance my studies while building a small business that focuses on dresses, shoes, and bags.

My work started from a love of helping people look and feel their best. I enjoy styling, curating pieces, and offering practical advice so customers leave confident and satisfied.

Running a business alongside school has taught me time management, customer service, and how to operate with limited resources while still delivering quality.
